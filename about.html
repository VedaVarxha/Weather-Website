Getting started:

Whether you're planning your day or preparing for an upcoming trip, our weather page has got you covered. Here's a quick guide to help you make the most of our weather forecast.

1. Location Search:

At the top right of the page, you'll find a search bar. Type in the name of your city to get started. Our weather page supports locations from around the world, so you can get accurate forecasts no matter where you are.

2. Current Weather:

As soon as you search for a location, you'll see the current weather conditions. This includes temperature, humidity, wind speed, and much more. Keep in mind that this information is updated regularly, so you'll always be informed about the latest weather changes.

3. Personalization:

Sign up for a free account to personalize your weather experience. You can save your favorite locations, set up notifications for specific weather conditions, and even customize the units for temperature, wind speed, and more.

4. FAQ and Help:

If you have any questions or need assistance, visit our FAQ and Help section. We've compiled a list of common queries to guide you through any issues you might encounter.
